A brief History

Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba (3 January 1996 - 12 September 2023), known professionally as MohBad, was a Nigerian rapper, singer, and songwriter from Lagos. He was formerly signed to Naira Marley's Marlian Records and left the label in 2022. He was best known for his hit songs "Ponmo," "Peace," "Beast and Peace," "Sorry", "Feel Good," and "KPK (Ko Por Ke)" with Rexxie, which was nominated three times for The Headies awards 2022. On 18 September 2023, he ranked number 1 on TurnTable's Official Artiste Top 100

Lifetime Achievements

  • Headies Award Nomination (Best Street-Hop Artist)
  • Ranked 12th amongst the Top 21 Afrobeat artists of 2021
  • City People Music Arward - Revelation of the Year(Nomination)
